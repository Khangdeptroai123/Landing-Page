Eucalyptus Charcoal

Eucalyptus Charcoal offers high heat and long burn time, perfect for BBQ grilling. Free samples, OEM services, and reliable exports to Taiwan, Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia, Bahrain, Kuwait, New Zealand, and China.

Eucalyptus Charcoal is widely preferred because of its smokeless, odorless feature. It produces high and consistent heat, making it the perfect charcoal for BBQ parties.

GROVA is proud to say that Eucalyptus Charcoal is our primary product. We are our own supplier of mangrove wood – the main material to make Eucalyptus Charcoal. With this considerable advantage, GROVA manages to supply Eucalyptus Charcoal in stable quantities at a competitive price.
